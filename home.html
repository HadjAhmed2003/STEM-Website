ABOUT US

The Algiers STEM Center is an industry-led initiative to strengthen the innovation, critical thinking and communication skills of the Algerian workforce via direct training of youth, targeted training of teachers and community education forums and events. The Boeing Company, Dow Chemical, Occidental Petroleum and the U.S. Embassy in Algiers support World Learning’s STEM initiatives in Algeria.
